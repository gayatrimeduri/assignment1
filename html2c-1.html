Carrot Cultivation

Carrots are grown by direct seed sowing in containers.
Sow seeds about 0.5 cm deep, and plant 2-3 seeds in each planting hole.
Firm the soil gently after covering the seeds and water them immediately. Seeds should germinate within 8-10 days.
Carrot is a winter season crop and if grown at 15°C to 20°C will develop a very good colour.
Carrot crop needs deep loose loamy soil and pH should be ranging from 6.0 to 7.0 for higher production.
You can easily grow this healthy vegetable in your kitchen garden, all you need is a little guidance as to when and how to grow it.
